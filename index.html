Masha

About Masha

Masha is a smart, kind, but mischievous little girl who loves exploring the world around her. This leads to many funny and entertaining situations.

Masha's charateristics

  1. Masha is Russian and lives in the forest with her pig, goat, and dog
  2. All the animals in the forest are afraid of her as she is constantly forcing them to play with her.
  3. Masha's friend Bear is always trying to keep Masha out of trouble, but often ends up the unintended victim of her mischief

This is Masha in action.

Masha's friends

  • Bear: The Bear is a retired circus bear who lives in a tree house in the forest. His past occupation makes him very talented in performing arts
  • Dasha: Dasha is Masha's cousin from Moscow. She looks like Masha but she is very different, she has beige hair, blue eyes, wears blue glasses and an orange dress.
  • Panda: Panda is a panda cub and the Bear's young cousin from China. He and Masha are rivals but they occasionally get along and have fun together.
